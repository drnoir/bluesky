Suzuki Baleno

The practical hatchback with a turbocharged engine. The new Suzuki Baleno is the five-door hatchback that’s also a joy to drive. With the Boosterjet turbocharged engine, you enjoy maximum performance while maintaining great fuel efficiency. And for a compact car, it’s still has plenty of room, boasting a remarkably spacious interior. Plus, it comes packed full of features, including satellite navigation and air conditioning as standard.
